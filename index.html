  • Financial need is total cost minus expected family contribution (determined from FAFSA and CSS profile).
  • Students are expected to contribute $2,000 (minimum summer earnings)
  • MIT fully meets financial need
  • Family contribution is calculated using Dept. of Education’s Federal Methodology formula
  • Students are expected to contribute $1,500 as freshmen and $1,900 as continuing students
