Cost of Attendance

  • Tuition: $48,111
  • Fees: $1,797
  • Housing: $8,391
  • Board: $6,405
  • Additional Meal Allowance: $900
  • Books/Supplies: $1,323
  • Personal Expenses: $1,974
  • Total: $68,901

Financial Need

  • Family contribution is calculated using Dept. of Education’s Federal Methodology formula
  • Students are expected to contribute $1,500 as freshmen and $1,900 as continuing students

Types of Aid

  • Grants and Scholarships (need based, don’t need to be repaid)
  • Work-Study (offerings from Caltech, public agencies like JPL, Caltech Y)
  • Loans (must be repaid)
